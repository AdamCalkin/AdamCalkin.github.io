Emotional Intelligence

What is Emotional Intelligence?
This is the emotional level you feel about yourself and how stable you feel yourself to be.

I can be looked at in many different ways and is very important in a person daily life.

How it is different to IQ?
IQ is your level of problem solving ability and how you look and problems and is you are smart enough to be able to figure out difficult problems.
But EQ has nothing to do with our over all intelligense in problem solving,
it is to do with how you feel. If you are happy with what you are doing and everthing is going fine emotionally
or if not what will you do to help your emotional level, such as meditaion,
stop doing the things you don't like doing and doing more of what you do like and taking breaks from work to relaxe in daily life.

Why is it important?
It is very important to keep your emotional level stable,
so you are able to do the things you like and can move on throughout life being happier.
If your emotional levels are low sometimes you just dont feel like doing anything or seeing anyone and could really put a toll on your health.
