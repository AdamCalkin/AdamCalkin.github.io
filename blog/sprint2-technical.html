What are the best practices associated with using classes vs. ids?
Classes and ids are in HTML because they allow us to edit specific sections of HTML in CSS.

Ids are indicated in CSS by a period '.' before the name of the id,
classes are indicated by a hash '#' before its name.

Classes are for being used all over the website and for making a section show how you want each page to be.

Ids are for a specific one thing on the page, for styling a section apart from the class.
